Vanilla Caramel Pudding

Soft, creamy, and silky-smooth vanilla pudding topped with golden caramel — a timeless dessert that melts in your mouth!

Ingredients


  • 1/2 cup sugar (for caramel)
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar (for custard)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt


Instructions


  1. Prepare the caramel: In a saucepan, melt 1/2 cup sugar over medium heat until it turns golden brown. Quickly pour the caramel into a pudding mold or several ramekins, swirling to coat the bottom. Set aside.
  2. Make the custard: In another pan, heat milk gently until warm (not boiling). In a bowl, whisk eggs, sugar, vanilla, and a pinch of salt together until smooth.
  3. Slowly pour the warm milk into the egg mixture while whisking constantly to avoid curdling.
  4. Strain the mixture through a fine sieve to make it silky smooth, then pour it over the set caramel in the mold.
  5. Place the mold in a baking tray. Fill the tray halfway with hot water (water bath method).
  6. Bake at 160°C (320°F) for 35-45 minutes, or until the pudding is set but slightly jiggly in the center.
  7. Cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  8. To serve, run a knife around the edge and invert the pudding onto a plate — watch that caramel glaze drip beautifully!
