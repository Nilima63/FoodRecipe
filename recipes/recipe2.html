Dalgona Coffee

Ingredients


  • 2 tbsp instant coffee
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp hot water
  • 1 cup cold or hot milk
  • Ice cubes (optional, for cold version)



Instructions


  1. In a bowl, add coffee, sugar, and hot water (equal amounts).
  2. Whisk or beat the mixture using a spoon, hand whisk, or electric beater for 3-5 minutes until it becomes thick, creamy, and foamy.
  3. Fill a glass with milk (cold or hot) and ice cubes if you like.
  4. Spoon the whipped coffee foam on top of the milk.
