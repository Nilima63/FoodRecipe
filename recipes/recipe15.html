Beef Chana Masala

A hearty, flavorful curry made with tender beef chunks and protein-rich chana dal simmered together in aromatic spices. This traditional dish is rich, comforting, and perfect to enjoy with naan or steamed rice!

Ingredients


  • 500g beef (boneless, cut into cubes)
  • 1 cup chana dal (Bengal gram)
  • 2 onions (finely sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies (optional)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)


Instructions


  1. Prepare dal: Soak chana dal in water for 1 hour, then boil until half-cooked. Drain and keep aside.
  2. Cook beef: Heat oil in a pressure cooker or pot. Add cumin seeds, then sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
  3. Add spices: Stir in ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add beef & yogurt: Add beef pieces, salt, and yogurt. Mix well and cook until the meat changes color and releases its juices.
  5. Simmer: Add chopped tomatoes, cover, and cook until soft. Add 1-1.5 cups of water and cook (pressure cook 15-20 mins or simmer until tender).
  6. Combine dal: Add the half-cooked chana dal to the beef curry. Stir well and cook for another 10-15 minutes until the dal and meat blend together beautifully.
  7. Finish: Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander.
  8. Serve hot: Pair with garlic naan, paratha, or plain basmati rice. Perfect for family dinners!
