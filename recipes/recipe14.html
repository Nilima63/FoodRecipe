Garlic Naan

Soft, buttery, and loaded with the aroma of fresh garlic — Garlic Naan is the perfect partner for your favorite curries! Slightly crisp on the edges yet pillowy in the center, this naan will melt in your mouth with every bite.

Ingredients


  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp oil or melted butter
  • 3/4 cup warm water (as needed)
  • 3 tbsp butter (for brushing)
  • 3 cloves garlic (finely minced)
  • Fresh coriander (chopped, for garnish)


Instructions


  1. Activate yeast: In a bowl, mix yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let it rest for 10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Make the dough: Add flour, salt, yogurt, and oil. Mix well and knead into a soft dough. Cover and let it rest for 1-1.5 hours until doubled in size.
  3. Prepare toppings: In a small bowl, mix melted butter with minced garlic and chopped coriander.
  4. Shape the naan: Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each one into an oval shape.
  5. Cook: Heat a pan or tawa over medium-high heat. Place naan (wet one side with water) on the pan — cook until bubbles appear. Flip and cook directly over flame or on the pan until golden brown.
  6. Finish: Brush the hot naan with garlic butter mixture. Sprinkle some coriander for extra freshness.
  7. Serve hot: Enjoy your garlic naan with butter chicken, paneer curry, or lentil soup!

Tip: You can bake Garlic Naan in an oven too — just preheat to 250°C and bake for 5-7 minutes until puffed and golden.
