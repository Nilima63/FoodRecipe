Fried Chicken

Crispy, golden, and juicy — homemade Fried Chicken is a classic comfort food. Marinated in spices and coated in a crunchy batter, it’s perfect for lunch, dinner, or a snack.

Ingredients


  • 1 kg chicken pieces (drumsticks, wings, thighs)
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Oil for deep frying


Instructions


  1. Marinate: In a bowl, combine chicken pieces with buttermilk, salt, black pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder. Cover and refrigerate for 2-4 hours (or overnight for best flavor).
  2. Coat: Place flour in a shallow bowl. Dredge each marinated chicken piece in flour, coating thoroughly.
  3. Heat oil: Pour enough oil into a deep frying pan or pot to submerge the chicken. Heat over medium-high flame until hot (around 175°C / 350°F).
  4. Fry: Carefully add chicken pieces to the hot oil. Fry in batches for 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden brown and fully cooked inside.
  5. Drain & serve: Remove chicken and place on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot with ketchup, spicy mayo, or your favorite dipping sauce!

Tip: For extra crispiness, double dredge the chicken in flour. Use a thermometer to ensure the chicken is cooked through (75°C / 165°F internally).
