Chicken Cheese Pasta

Creamy, cheesy, and loaded with tender chicken, this Chicken Cheese Pasta is a comforting and hearty meal. Perfect for lunch, dinner, or even a weekend treat!

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (penne, fusilli, or spaghetti)
  • 200g chicken breast (cut into cubes)
  • 1 cup grated cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta: Boil pasta in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook chicken: Heat olive oil in a pan. Add chicken cubes, season with salt and pepper, and cook until golden and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. Make cheese sauce: In the same pan, melt butter. Add minced garlic and chopped onions, sauté until soft. Stir in flour and cook for 1 minute. Gradually add milk, whisking continuously to avoid lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  4. Add cheese: Lower the heat and add grated cheese. Stir until melted and smooth. Season with pepper and chili flakes (if using).
  5. Combine: Add cooked chicken and pasta into the sauce. Mix well to coat evenly.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh parsley and extra grated cheese if desired. Serve hot!

Tip: For a creamier version, add 2-3 tbsp of cream. You can also toss in vegetables like bell peppers or mushrooms for extra flavor.
