Egg Potato Cutlets

Crispy on the outside and soft inside — these Egg Potato Cutlets are the perfect evening snack! Made with mashed potatoes, eggs, and spices, they’re simple, filling, and absolutely delicious.

Ingredients


  • 3 medium potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 2 boiled eggs (chopped)
  • 1 raw egg (for coating)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • ½ tsp black pepper powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ cup breadcrumbs (for coating)
  • Oil for frying


Instructions


  1. Prepare the mixture: In a bowl, mix mashed potatoes, chopped boiled eggs, onions, chilies, coriander, ginger-garlic paste, pepper, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Shape: Take small portions of the mixture and shape them into round or oval cutlets.
  3. Coat: Beat the raw egg in a small bowl. Dip each cutlet into the beaten egg, then roll in breadcrumbs to coat evenly.
  4. Fry: Heat oil in a frying pan on medium flame. Fry the cutlets until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  5. Drain & serve: Remove from oil and place on paper towels to absorb extra oil.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot with tomato ketchup, mint chutney, or spicy mayo!

Tip: You can bake or air-fry the cutlets for a healthier version. For extra crispiness, double coat with egg and breadcrumbs.
