Chicken Cheese Sandwich

A quick, tasty, and filling sandwich packed with juicy chicken, melted cheese, and a hint of spices — perfect for breakfast, lunch, or an evening snack. Crispy on the outside and creamy inside, this sandwich is everyone's favorite!

Ingredients


  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella or cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1/4 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Butter for toasting
  • 4-6 slices of bread
  • Salt to taste


Instructions


  1. Prepare the filling: In a bowl, mix shredded chicken, chopped onion, mayonnaise, grated cheese, black pepper, chili flakes, and salt. Stir well until everything is evenly coated.
  2. Assemble the sandwich: Spread butter on one side of each bread slice. On the unbuttered side, add 2-3 tablespoons of chicken-cheese mixture and cover with another slice (buttered side facing out).
  3. Toast or grill: Heat a pan or sandwich maker. Place the sandwich and toast both sides until golden brown and crispy, and the cheese has melted beautifully inside.
  4. Serve hot: Cut diagonally, serve with ketchup, chili sauce, or a cup of hot coffee — and enjoy your cheesy delight!
