Swiss Roll Cake

Soft, fluffy, and perfectly rolled — this Swiss Roll Cake is a delightful dessert for any occasion. Filled with whipped cream and your favorite jam or chocolate, it’s light, airy, and absolutely irresistible.

Ingredients


  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g (½ cup) sugar
  • 100g (¾ cup) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)
  • Filling: ½ cup whipped cream + 2-3 tbsp jam or chocolate spread


Instructions


  1. Preheat: Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Line a 10x15 inch baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Beat eggs & sugar: In a bowl, whisk eggs and sugar until pale, fluffy, and tripled in volume.
  3. Add vanilla & flour: Gently fold in vanilla extract, sifted flour, pinch of salt, and milk. Mix carefully to keep the batter airy.
  4. Bake: Pour batter into prepared tray and spread evenly. Bake for 12-15 minutes until golden and springy to touch.
  5. Roll: While hot, flip the cake onto a clean kitchen towel sprinkled with powdered sugar. Remove parchment and roll the cake gently with the towel. Cool completely.
  6. Fill: Unroll the cake, spread whipped cream and jam/chocolate evenly, then roll back carefully.
  7. Serve: Dust with powdered sugar, slice, and enjoy your Swiss Roll Cake!

Tip: Use chilled whipped cream for a more stable filling. You can also add fresh fruits for extra flavor.
