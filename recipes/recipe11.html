Coffee Pudding

Smooth, creamy, and rich with the bold aroma of coffee — this Coffee Pudding is a perfect dessert for coffee lovers! Lightly sweet, silky in texture, and topped with whipped cream or chocolate flakes — it's pure indulgence in every spoonful!

Ingredients


  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp instant coffee powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch (or custard powder)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream (for topping)
  • Chocolate shavings or cocoa powder (for garnish)


Instructions


  1. Mix the ingredients: In a small bowl, combine cornstarch with 1/4 cup of milk and mix until smooth. Set aside.
  2. Heat the milk: In a saucepan, add the rest of the milk, sugar, and coffee powder. Stir continuously over medium heat until the sugar and coffee dissolve completely.
  3. Thicken the pudding: Slowly pour the cornstarch mixture into the hot milk while stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Cook until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon.
  4. Add flavor: Turn off the heat, add vanilla extract, and stir well.
  5. Set and chill: Pour the pudding into serving bowls or cups. Let it cool, then refrigerate for 2-3 hours or until set.
  6. Decorate and serve: Top with whipped cream, sprinkle chocolate shavings or a pinch of cocoa powder — and enjoy your creamy coffee delight!
