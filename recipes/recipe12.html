Moist Vanilla Cake

Light, fluffy, and perfectly moist — this Vanilla Cake is pure happiness in every bite! A simple yet elegant dessert that melts in your mouth, topped with creamy frosting or served plain with tea. Perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or just because you deserve cake!

Ingredients


  • 2 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 and 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk (room temperature)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil (for extra moisture)


Instructions


  1. Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line two 8-inch round baking pans with parchment paper.
  2. Mix dry ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. Cream butter and sugar: In another large bowl, beat butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  4. Add vanilla & wet ingredients: Stir in vanilla extract and vegetable oil. Gradually add the dry ingredients alternately with milk, mixing until smooth and creamy.
  5. Bake: Divide the batter evenly between pans. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool & frost: Let cakes cool completely before frosting or serving. You can use whipped cream, buttercream, or simply dust with powdered sugar!
  7. Enjoy: Slice, serve, and enjoy your soft, moist vanilla cake — every bite feels like a warm hug!
