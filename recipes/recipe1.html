Spring Rolls

Instructions


  1. Prepare filling: Heat sesame oil in a pan. SautÃ© garlic, then add cabbage, carrots, bell peppers, and cooked chicken if using. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes. Add soy sauce and pepper. Let the mixture cool.
  2. Assemble spring rolls: Place a wrapper on a flat surface. Spoon 2-3 tbsp of filling near the edge. Fold sides and roll tightly. Seal the edge with a little water.
  3. Fry: Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Fry spring rolls in batches on medium heat until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
  4. Serve: Serve hot with sweet chili sauce or soy sauce.
